About

Welcome to my online sports blog! I can’t wait to share a lot of interesting stories with all my readers!,

Hi, I’m Josh Lill! I’m currently a sophomore at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, double-majoring in broadcasting and sports media communications. My goal in life is to be a sports announcer, but I love blogging and writing about sports as well. Not only am I a huge fan of the MLB, NFL, NBA and NHL, but I am a die-hard fan of the Chicago Cubs, Bears, Bulls and Blackhawks. With this blog, I hope to take the sports knowledge that I have gained over the last 10 years to help inform and entertain readers with interesting facts and stories occurring in the world of sports.

I hope any readers who come across this blog enjoy it!
